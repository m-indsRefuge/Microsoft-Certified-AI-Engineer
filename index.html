My Microsoft AI Engineer Certification Story

This certification is designed for engineers and developers who want to build intelligent applications using Azure AI services.
Whether you're a machine learning practitioner, a cloud architect, or a developer exploring AI integration.

Certification Overview

As a Microsoft Azure AI engineer, you build, manage, and deploy AI solutions that leverage Azure AI. Your responsibilities include participating in all phases of AI solutions development, including:

  • Requirements definition and design
  • Development
  • Deployment
  • Integration
  • Maintenance
  • Performance tuning
  • Monitoring

You should be able to use Representational State Transfer (REST) APIs and SDKs to build secure image processing, video processing, natural language processing, knowledge mining, and generative AI solutions on Azure.

Skills Measured

  • Plan and manage Azure AI solutions
  • Implement Generative AI solutions
  • Implement Agentic solutions
  • Implement Computer Vision solutions
  • Implement Natural Language Processing (NLP) solutions
  • Implement knowledge mining and document intelligence solutions

A Brief History of Artificial Intelligence

AI’s story spans decades of breakthroughs. In the 1950s, researchers first asked if machines could think, sparking symbolic AI and logic-based reasoning. By the ’70s and ’80s, expert systems emerged, rule-based programs that mirrored human specialists in specific fields. In the ’90s and 2000s, machine learning shifted the paradigm, enabling systems to learn from data rather than rely solely on fixed rules. The 2010s saw deep learning and neural networks revolutionize speech, vision, and language understanding. Now, in the 2020s, generative AI and cloud platforms like Microsoft Azure are making powerful models accessible to all, driving innovation from conversational assistants to industrial automation. The journey is far from over, AI continues to push the boundaries of what machines can understand and create.
